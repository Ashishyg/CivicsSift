Program Details:

  • Program Name: Crop Insurance Program
  • Target Audience: Farmers with cultivated land
  • Objective: Provides insurance coverage for crops

Benefits:

  • Compensation for Crop Losses: Financial support in case of crop losses due to natural disasters.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to farmers with actively cultivated land.

Program Period:

  • Active from June 1, 2024, to May 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides farmers with insurance coverage for their crops, ensuring compensation for losses incurred due to natural disasters, as facilitated by the Ministry of Agriculture.
