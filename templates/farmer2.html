Program Details:

  • Program Name: Modern Agricultural Equipment Subsidy
  • Target Audience: Farmers in need of agricultural machinery
  • Objective: Subsidizes the purchase of modern farming equipment

Benefits:

  • Reduced Cost of Agricultural Machinery: Subsidy to decrease the financial burden on farmers for purchasing modern farming equipment.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to farmers in need of agricultural machinery.

Program Period:

  • Active from July 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.

Participation during this period provides farmers with a subsidy to reduce the cost of purchasing modern agricultural equipment, supporting the adoption of advanced farming practices, as facilitated by the Ministry of Agriculture.
