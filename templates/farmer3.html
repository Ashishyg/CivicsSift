Program Details:

  • Program Name: Soil Health Improvement Initiative
  • Target Audience: Farmers interested in soil health
  • Objective: Promotes soil health and fertility improvement

Benefits:

  • Free Soil Testing: Access to complimentary soil testing services.
  • Subsidies for Soil Amendments: Financial support for acquiring soil amendments to improve soil fertility.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to farmers actively interested in soil health improvement.

Program Period:

  • Active from August 1, 2024, to July 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides farmers with free soil testing and subsidies for soil amendments, promoting soil health and fertility improvement, as facilitated by the Ministry of Agriculture.
