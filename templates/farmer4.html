Program Details:

  • Program Name: Drought-Resistant Crop Development
  • Target Audience: Farmers in drought-prone regions
  • Objective: Encourages cultivation of drought-resistant crops

Benefits:

  • Seeds and Support: Provision of seeds and support for cultivating drought-resistant crop varieties.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to farmers in regions prone to drought.

Program Period:

  • Active from September 1, 2024, to August 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides farmers in drought-prone regions with seeds and support to cultivate drought-resistant crop varieties, encouraging sustainable agriculture practices, as facilitated by the Ministry of Agriculture.
