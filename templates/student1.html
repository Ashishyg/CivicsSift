Program Details:

  • Program Name: Education for All
  • Target Audience: Students pursuing primary, secondary, or higher education
  • Objective: Provides financial assistance and support for educational expenses

Benefits:

  • Scholarship Funds: Financial support for educational expenses.
  • Educational Resources: Access to resources to aid in academic success.
  • Mentorship Programs: Guidance and mentorship opportunities for academic and personal development.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to students actively pursuing primary, secondary, or higher education.

Program Period:

  • Active from June 1, 2024, to May 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides students with financial assistance, educational resources, and mentorship opportunities, promoting inclusive education for all, as facilitated by the Ministry of Education.
