Program Details:

  • Program Name: Skill Development for Students
  • Target Audience: Students seeking vocational training and skill development
  • Objective: Facilitates training programs for employable skills

Benefits:

  • Vocational Training: Specialized training programs for acquiring employable skills.
  • Job Placement Assistance: Support in finding suitable employment opportunities.
  • Career Counseling: Guidance for students to make informed career choices.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to students actively seeking vocational training and skill development.

Program Period:

  • Active from August 1, 2024, to July 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides students with the opportunity to undergo vocational training, receive job placement assistance, and benefit from career counseling services, as facilitated by the Ministry of Skill Development.
