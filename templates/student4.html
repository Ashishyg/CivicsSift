Program Details:

  • Program Name: Digital Literacy Initiative
  • Target Audience: Students aiming to enhance digital skills
  • Objective: Promotes digital literacy and technological proficiency

Benefits:

  • Training in Digital Technologies: Access to specialized training in digital skills and technologies.
  • Access to Digital Resources: Resources to enhance digital literacy and proficiency.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to students actively aiming to enhance their digital skills.

Program Period:

  • Active from September 1, 2024, to August 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides students with the opportunity to receive training in digital technologies and access resources that promote digital literacy and technological proficiency, as facilitated by the Ministry of Information Technology.
