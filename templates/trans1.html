Program Details:

  • Program Name: Transgender Education Scholarship
  • Target Audience: Transgender individuals pursuing higher education
  • Objective: Provides financial assistance for tuition and living expenses

Benefits:

  • Scholarship Funds: Financial support for tuition and living expenses.
  • Mentorship Programs: Guidance and mentorship opportunities for transgender scholars.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to transgender individuals actively pursuing higher education.

Program Period:

  • Active from June 1, 2024, to May 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides transgender individuals with crucial financial assistance and mentorship opportunities, fostering their pursuit of higher education, as facilitated by the Ministry of Education.
