Program Details:

  • Program Name: Healthcare Equality Program
  • Target Audience: All transgender individuals
  • Objective: Ensures access to gender-affirming healthcare services

Benefits:

  • Medical Consultations: Access to healthcare professionals for medical advice.
  • Hormone Therapy: Provision of hormone therapy for gender affirmation.
  • Gender-Affirming Surgeries: Support for gender-affirming surgeries as needed.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to all transgender individuals seeking gender-affirming healthcare services.

Program Period:

  • Active from July 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.

Participation during this period ensures that all transgender individuals have equal access to gender-affirming healthcare services, including medical consultations, hormone therapy, and gender-affirming surgeries, as provided by the Ministry of Health.
