Program Details:

  • Program Name: Transgender Employment Support
  • Target Audience: Unemployed transgender individuals
  • Objective: Assists in finding employment opportunities and skill development

Benefits:

  • Job Placement Services: Support in finding suitable employment opportunities.
  • Vocational Training Programs: Skill development programs for increased employability.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to unemployed transgender individuals seeking employment assistance.

Program Period:

  • Active from August 1, 2024, to July 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides unemployed transgender individuals with valuable support in finding employment opportunities and acquiring necessary skills, as facilitated by the Ministry of Labor.
