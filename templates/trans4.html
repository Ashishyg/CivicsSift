Program Details:

  • Program Name: Housing Rights for Transgender Individuals
  • Target Audience: Transgender individuals facing housing discrimination or homelessness
  • Objective: Provides housing assistance and legal support

Benefits:

  • Temporary Shelters: Safe and temporary housing solutions for individuals facing homelessness.
  • Rental Assistance: Financial support for securing stable housing.
  • Legal Aid: Assistance with legal matters related to housing discrimination.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to transgender individuals facing housing discrimination or homelessness.

Program Period:

  • Active from September 1, 2024, to August 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides transgender individuals facing housing challenges with temporary shelters, rental assistance, and legal aid, as facilitated by the Ministry of Social Justice.
