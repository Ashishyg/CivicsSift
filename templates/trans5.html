Program Details:

  • Program Name: Transgender Cultural Empowerment Grant
  • Target Audience: Transgender individuals involved in promoting cultural awareness
  • Objective: Supports cultural projects and events that celebrate transgender diversity

Benefits:

  • Grant Funding: Financial support for organizing cultural events, workshops, and artistic projects.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to transgender individuals actively involved in promoting cultural awareness.

Program Period:

  • Active from October 1, 2024, to May 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides transgender individuals with the opportunity to receive grant funding for cultural projects and events that celebrate transgender diversity, as facilitated by the Ministry of Culture.
