Women's Empowerment in Agriculture

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Women's Empowerment in Agriculture
  • Target Audience: Women involved in agriculture
  • Objective: Promotes sustainable farming practices and market access

Benefits:

  • Training: Skill development programs for sustainable farming practices.
  • Agricultural Resources: Access to essential resources for improved agricultural productivity.
  • Market Linkages: Assistance in connecting women farmers to broader market opportunities.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to women actively involved in agriculture.

Program Period:

  • Active from April 1, 2025, to April 30, 2025.

Participation during this period empowers women in agriculture with valuable resources and support from the Ministry of Agriculture, fostering sustainable farming practices and market access.
