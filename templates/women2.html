Maternal Healthcare Support

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Maternal Healthcare Support
  • Target Audience: Pregnant women and new mothers
  • Objective: Ensures access to quality maternal healthcare services

Benefits:

  • Prenatal Care: Comprehensive healthcare services during pregnancy.
  • Postnatal Care: Support and healthcare services for new mothers after childbirth.
  • Vaccinations: Immunization programs to safeguard the health of both mothers and newborns.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to pregnant women and new mothers.

Application Period:

  • Active from July 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.

Enrollment during this period ensures access to crucial maternal healthcare support provided by the Ministry of Health.
