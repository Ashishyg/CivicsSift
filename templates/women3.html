Women's Entrepreneurship Development

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Women's Entrepreneurship Development
  • Target Audience: Aspiring women entrepreneurs
  • Objective: Facilitates skill development and business support

Benefits:

  • Training Programs: Skill development initiatives tailored for aspiring women entrepreneurs.
  • Startup Grants: Financial support to kickstart entrepreneurial ventures.
  • Mentorship: Access to mentorship programs for guidance and support in business development.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to aspiring women entrepreneurs.

Application Period:

  • Active from August 1, 2024, to July 31, 2025.

Enrollment during this period provides aspiring women entrepreneurs with valuable resources and support from the Ministry of Small and Medium Enterprises.
