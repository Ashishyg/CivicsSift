Gender Equality in the Workplace

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Gender Equality in the Workplace
  • Target Audience: Working women facing discrimination
  • Objective: Promotes gender equality and addresses workplace bias

Benefits:

  • Equal Pay Initiatives: Measures to ensure fair and equal pay for all employees.
  • Anti-Discrimination Training: Educational programs to raise awareness and combat workplace bias.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to working women facing discrimination in the workplace.

Application Period:

  • Active from September 1, 2024, to August 31, 2025.

Enrollment during this period provides working women with essential resources and support from the Ministry of Labor to address workplace discrimination and promote gender equality.
