Rural Women Development Fund

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Rural Women Development Fund
  • Target Audience: Rural women involved in agriculture or cottage industries
  • Objective: Supports rural women in economic activities

Benefits:

  • Agricultural Training: Skill development programs tailored for rural women in agriculture.
  • Financial Aid: Support through financial assistance for economic empowerment.
  • Infrastructure Development: Initiatives to enhance rural infrastructure for economic activities.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to rural women actively involved in agriculture or cottage industries.

Application Period:

  • Active from October 1, 2024, to May 31, 2025.

Enrollment during this period provides rural women with crucial resources and support from the Ministry of Rural Development to enhance their economic activities and livelihoods.
