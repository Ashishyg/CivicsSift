Women's Health Awareness Program

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Women's Health Awareness Program
  • Target Audience: All women in the community
  • Objective: Promotes health awareness and preventive care

Benefits:

  • Health Screenings: Access to regular health screenings for preventive care.
  • Workshops: Educational workshops on women's health and well-being.
  • Informational Campaigns: Awareness campaigns to disseminate crucial health information.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to all women in the community.

Program Period:

  • Active from November 1, 2024, to November 30, 2024.

Participation during this period empowers women in the community with valuable health knowledge and resources provided by the Ministry of Health.
