Skill Development for Single Mothers

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Skill Development for Single Mothers
  • Target Audience: Single mothers and caregivers
  • Objective: Provides training for employable skills

Benefits:

  • Vocational Training: Specialized training programs for skill development.
  • Job Placement Assistance: Support in finding employment opportunities.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to single mothers and caregivers.

Program Period:

  • Active from January 1, 2025, to January 31, 2025.

Participation during this period empowers single mothers and caregivers with valuable skills and job placement assistance provided by the Ministry of Women and Child Development.
