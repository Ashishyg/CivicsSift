Domestic Violence Support Helpline

Program Details:

  • Program Name: Domestic Violence Support Helpline
  • Target Audience: Women facing domestic violence
  • Objective: Provides a helpline for counseling and support

Benefits:

  • Crisis Intervention: Immediate support and assistance during domestic violence crises.
  • Legal Aid Referrals: Guidance and referrals for legal assistance to address domestic violence issues.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to women facing domestic violence seeking counseling and support.

Program Period:

  • Active from March 1, 2025, to March 31, 2025.

Participation during this period provides women facing domestic violence with immediate counseling and support through the helpline services offered by the Ministry of Social Justice.
